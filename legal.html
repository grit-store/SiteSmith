Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

At OpenCurb Media, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and safeguard the information you provide when you interact with our website, subscribe to our service retainers, or collaborate with us on digital agency projects.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily share, as well as automatic technical telemetry:

  • Account & Registration Details: When you subscribe to our plans, we collect contact credentials including name, email address, company name, phone number, and billing information (handled securely through our payment processors).
  • Project Submissions & Code Files: Any assets, project descriptions, wireframes, repository keys, database credentials, or architectural designs you provide us during onboarding.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized analytical parameters such as browser details, access timestamps, visited pages, IP addresses, and interaction metrics on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your details are processed to execute our services, maintain accounts, and secure operational infrastructure:

  • To deliver frontend, interactive frontend, or full-stack software development under subscription plans.
  • To handle billing cycles, process payments, and send transactional notifications.
  • To optimize website responsiveness, conduct diagnostic testing, and defend against fraudulent activities.
  • To communicate updates about your project deliverables, schedule review sessions, and provide developer support.

3. Data Security and Code Ownership Protection

Because we construct mission-critical websites and application source code, security is our primary focus:

We enforce restricted access logs, encrypted file exchanges, and secure password managers. Any access tokens or keys provided for deployment environments are deleted immediately upon subscription termination or staging completion. We do not store client database credentials in plain text.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent client data or source code. We share data only in these narrow scopes:

  • Service Providers: Standard third-party tools to support operations (e.g., Stripe for billing, Web3Forms/Email infrastructure, GitHub for repository hosting).
  • Legal Compliance: When required by binding legal orders, court summons, or regulatory inquiries.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies and minor analytical identifiers to improve visitor experiences. You can disable cookies inside your local browser settings, though doing so might disrupt session continuity on specific payment pages.

6. Your Rights & GDPR Compliance

Under international frameworks like GDPR and CCPA, clients retain complete authority to access, edit, restrict, or purge their personal accounts. If you wish to request total deletion of your profile history or active developer credentials, contact us at the address below.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please email our legal team at:

legal@opencore.media
